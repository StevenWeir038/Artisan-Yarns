About the Newsletter App

This page was created out of habit. As a side thought, wouldn't it be better to give the user the ability to subscribe from any page of the site?

The solution could be to place the feature in the footer. It is included in base.html template which can easily be extended to any page.

Signing up to something can be considered a passive decision so the more someone is nudged, the more likely they are to perform an action.

This is a convention I stumbled upon during the wireframing process but thought it may be useful to leave this reflection on the site for other developers.

It is unlikely a standard web user would to access this page so there should be no harm in its admission.

It was also a good exercise to pass form objects through a context processor rather than just a view to make it available to all templates by default rather than having to declare on each.
