About the Reviews App

The reviews app presents a similar problem to the newsletter app. The newsletter form object needed to be available to the whole site via the base.html footer (beyond the app). Therefore it was placed in a context processor to act like a project level view.

Models and forms associated with the reviews app are actually to be used within the products app, more specifically the product_detail template.

Rather than pass through a contect processor, just import the app object. Template tags can do the rest...

I opted to keep reviews as its own app due to separation of concern.
